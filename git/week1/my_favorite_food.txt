Ingredients:

3 eggs
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
Step-by-Step:

Beat the eggs with the teaspoon of salt.

Add the cup of flour and the tablespoon of sugar, and slowly add the milk.

Let the mixture rest in the fridge for 30 minutes, and it’s ready.
